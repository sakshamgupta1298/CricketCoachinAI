Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated:

Introduction

At CrickCoach AI ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your right to control your personal data. This Data Deletion Policy explains how you can request the deletion of your account and associated data, what information will be deleted, and what information may be retained for legal or operational purposes.

This policy is designed to comply with data protection regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws. By using our Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

Your Right to Delete

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data and account at any time. This includes:

  • Your account profile and registration information
  • All uploaded cricket technique videos
  • Analysis results and performance metrics
  • Usage data and activity history
  • Any other personal information associated with your account

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion through the following methods:

Method 1: Through the Mobile App

  1. Open the CrickCoach AI mobile application
  2. Navigate to your Profile or Settings section
  3. Select "Account Settings" or "Privacy Settings"
  4. Tap on "Delete Account" or "Request Account Deletion"
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your deletion request
  6. You may be asked to enter your password or verify your identity

Method 2: Via Email

Send an email to admin@crickcoachai.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name (as registered)
  • A clear statement requesting account deletion
  • Any additional information that may help us verify your identity

Note: For security purposes, we may require additional verification before processing your deletion request, especially if you request deletion via email.

What Gets Deleted

Upon successful account deletion, we will permanently delete the following information:

Personal Information

  • Name and email address
  • Organization or academy name (if provided)
  • Contact information
  • Account credentials and authentication tokens

Video Content

  • All uploaded cricket technique videos
  • Video metadata (duration, file size, format information)
  • Recorded videos from the app

Analysis Data

  • All AI-generated analysis results
  • Performance metrics and statistics
  • Improvement recommendations and feedback
  • Progress tracking data

Usage Data

  • App usage patterns and activity logs
  • Feature access history
  • Device information associated with your account

What May Be Retained

In certain circumstances, we may be required to retain some information even after account deletion. This includes:

Legal and Compliance Requirements

  • Information required to comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government regulations
  • Records necessary for resolving disputes or enforcing our Terms of Service
  • Financial transaction records (if applicable) as required by law

Anonymized and Aggregated Data

  • Anonymized data that cannot be linked back to you personally
  • Aggregated analytics data used for service improvement
  • Statistical information that does not identify individual users

Backup Systems

  • Data stored in backup systems may take additional time to be fully deleted (up to 90 days)
  • We maintain secure backups for disaster recovery purposes

Important: Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be undone. You will lose access to all your videos, analysis results, and progress data permanently. Please ensure you have downloaded or saved any information you wish to keep before requesting deletion.

Deletion Timeline

We process deletion requests as follows:

  • Immediate: Your account will be deactivated immediately upon confirmation of your deletion request
  • Within 30 days: All personal data and associated content will be permanently deleted from our active systems
  • Within 90 days: Data will be removed from all backup systems and archives

You will receive a confirmation email once your account deletion has been processed. If you do not receive confirmation within 30 days, please contact us at admin@crickcoachai.com.

Partial Data Deletion

If you do not wish to delete your entire account but want to remove specific data, you can:

  • Delete Individual Videos: You can delete specific videos from your account through the app's video management features
  • Clear Analysis History: Remove specific analysis results while keeping your account active
  • Update Profile Information: Modify or remove specific personal information from your profile

For assistance with partial data deletion, please contact us at admin@crickcoachai.com.

Data Deletion for Coaches and Academies

If you are part of a coaching program or academy:

  • Deleting your account will remove your access to the platform
  • Your coach or academy administrator may retain copies of analysis data if you have granted them access
  • You should contact your coach or academy directly if you want them to delete any shared data
  • We will notify your coach or academy of your account deletion if you are part of an active program

Third-Party Services

Please note that if you have shared content from our Service on third-party platforms (social media, cloud storage, etc.), you will need to delete that content separately. We are not responsible for data stored on third-party services.

Children's Data

If you are a parent or guardian and wish to delete an account created by a child under 13, please contact us immediately at admin@crickcoachai.com. We will process the deletion request promptly and in accordance with applicable children's privacy laws.

Verification Process

To protect your privacy and security, we may require verification before processing a deletion request. This may include:

  • Confirming your email address
  • Verifying your identity through security questions
  • Requesting additional documentation in certain cases

We will only request information necessary to verify your identity and will not use this information for any other purpose.

Reactivation After Deletion

Once your account and data have been deleted, this action is permanent and cannot be reversed. If you wish to use CrickCoach AI again in the future, you will need to create a new account and start fresh.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Deletion Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification for significant changes (if you have an active account)

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we handle data deletion requests.
