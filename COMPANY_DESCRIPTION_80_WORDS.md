# CrickCoach AI - 80 Word Description

**CrickCoach AI** is a mobile application providing AI-powered cricket coaching and technique analysis services. Users record or upload videos of themselves batting or bowling, and the application uses artificial intelligence to analyze their cricket technique, identify technical flaws, compare performance against established coaching benchmarks, and provide personalized improvement recommendations and customized training plans. The application serves cricket players at all skill levels, from beginners to professionals, tracks progress over time, and makes professional coaching insights easily accessible through mobile technology.
