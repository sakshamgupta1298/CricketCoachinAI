# CrickCoach - Company Business Description

## Business Overview

**CrickCoach** is a mobile application that provides AI-powered cricket coaching and technique analysis to cricket players of all skill levels. The application leverages artificial intelligence and computer vision technology to offer personalized coaching feedback, technique improvement recommendations, and progress tracking.

## What CrickCoach Does

CrickCoach is a comprehensive cricket coaching mobile application that uses artificial intelligence to analyze cricket players' techniques and provide personalized coaching feedback. Users record or upload a video of themselves performing cricket actions—batting, bowling, or wicket-keeping—and select the relevant context (e.g. shot type for batsmen, bowler type, or keeping type). The application then analyzes their movements using pose detection and AI: it evaluates body positioning, movement patterns, and biomechanics, compares the player's technique against established coaching benchmarks, and identifies specific technical flaws with precise measurements. (The app does not use automatic action recognition; users specify the type of action in the video.) Based on this analysis, CrickCoach provides actionable recommendations, suggests specific practice drills, and offers general improvement tips tailored to each player's unique technique. The application also tracks progress over time by maintaining a complete history of all analyses, and generates customized training plans with structured practice schedules. CrickCoach serves batsmen, bowlers, and wicket-keepers; accommodates left- and right-handed players; supports bowling styles (fast, spin) and keeping types (e.g. standing up, standing back, diving catch, stumping); and is designed for players at all skill levels.

## Target Market

- Cricket players at all skill levels (beginner to professional)
- Cricket coaches and training academies
- Cricket clubs and teams
- Parents of young cricket players
- Cricket enthusiasts seeking to improve their game

## Value Proposition

CrickCoach makes professional-level cricket technique analysis accessible and affordable to everyone through mobile technology, eliminating the need for expensive coaching sessions or specialized equipment. Players can receive instant, detailed feedback on their technique anytime, anywhere, helping them improve their game more effectively and efficiently.

## Core Services

1. **Video-Based Technique Analysis**: Users upload or record videos of their cricket performance for AI-powered analysis
2. **Biomechanical Assessment**: Comprehensive evaluation of body movements, positioning, and technique
3. **Personalized Feedback**: Customized recommendations and improvement suggestions based on individual analysis
4. **Progress Tracking**: Historical record of all analyses with comparison capabilities
5. **Training Plan Generation**: Automated creation of structured practice schedules tailored to identified needs
