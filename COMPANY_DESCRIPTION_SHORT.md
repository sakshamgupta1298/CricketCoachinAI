# CrickCoach - Short Company Description

## Business Description (Short Version)

**CrickCoach** is a mobile application that provides AI-powered cricket coaching and technique analysis services. Users record or upload videos of themselves batting or bowling, and the application uses artificial intelligence to analyze their cricket technique, identify technical flaws, compare performance against coaching benchmarks, and provide personalized improvement recommendations and training plans. The application serves cricket players at all skill levels, tracks progress over time, and makes professional coaching insights accessible through mobile technology.
